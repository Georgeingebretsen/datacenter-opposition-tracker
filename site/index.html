About This Project

What is this?

An open database tracking every known instance of community opposition to datacenter development in the United States. From moratoria to lawsuits to grassroots protests, we document the growing movement of communities pushing back against rapid datacenter expansion.

Why does it matter?

Datacenters are being built at an unprecedented rate to power AI, consuming massive amounts of water, electricity, and land. Communities across America are organizing to demand accountability, transparency, and environmental protections. This database helps connect those efforts.

Data Sources

Compiled from Data Center Watch, Robert Bryce's reporting, FracTracker Alliance, Change.org petitions, local news coverage, and direct research.
